Rice Pudding

This rice pudding is the best I've ever tasted, and it gets rave reviews from everyone who tastes it! Sprinkle with nutmeg or cinnamon, if desired.

For creamier pudding, use short or medium-grain rice.

Rice pudding is a creamy mixture of rice and milk that is cooked until it's thick and soft. It can be sweet or savory.

This raisin-studded dessert rice pudding is thickened with an egg, sweetened with sugar, and enhanced with vanilla.

Ingredients

  • Rice: This creamy rice pudding recipe starts with white rice cooked until tender.
  • Milk: You'll need two cups of milk, divided.
  • Sugar:The rice pudding is sweetened with white sugar.
  • Salt:A pinch of salt enhances the flavor of the other ingredients, but it won't make the pudding taste salty.
  • Eggs:An egg adds moisture and helps bind the pudding together.
  • Cinammon:The finish ingredient
  • Butter:Butter adds even more moisture and richness.
  • Vanilla:A dash of vanilla enhances the overall flavor of the rice pudding.

Measures

  • 1 ½ cups cold water
  • ¾ cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 cups milk, divided
  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Step1

    2. Pour water into a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat; stir in rice.

    Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, about 20 minutes.

  2. Step2

    3. Combine cooked rice, 1 1/2 cups milk, sugar, and salt in a clean saucepan.

    Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until thick and creamy, about 15 minutes.

  3. Step3

    4. Stir in remaining 1/2 cup milk, beaten egg, and raisins; cook 2 minutes more, stirring constantly.

    Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla until combined; serve warm.
